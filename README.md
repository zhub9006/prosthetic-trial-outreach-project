## Top 5 Outreach Strategies

1. **Community-Based Screening Events** — Mobile screening at community centers, churches, and VA facilities in underserved areas
2. **Tele-Trial Participation Program** — Hybrid trial model combining in-person visits with telehealth
3. **Industry-Academic Partnership Pipeline** — Matching prosthetic manufacturers with underserved trial sites
4. **Diversity & Inclusion Benchmarks** — Mandate demographic reporting; set minimum 30% enrollment from underserved populations
5. **Patient Navigation & Transportation Services** — Dedicated navigator + CTA reduced-fare cards and Divvy adapted cycles
