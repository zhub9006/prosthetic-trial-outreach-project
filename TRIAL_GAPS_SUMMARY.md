## Top 7 Gaps for Underserved Groups

### 1. Racial/Ethnic Diversity
- Racial/ethnic breakdowns rarely reported in trial protocols
- Only 1 of 10 reviewed trials (NCT04427007) reported demographics — enrolled 100% White participants

### 2. Lower-Limb Specificity
- Some trials overlap with upper-limb prosthetics
- Many focus on transtibial only, excluding transfemoral users

### 3. Rural Access
- Most trial sites in major metros (Chicago, Boston, UK, France)
- No trials based in rural Midwest or Southern US

### 4. Age Range
- Many trials restrict to ages 18–60 or 18–65
- Excludes older adults who represent large portion of amputee population

### 5. Female Representation
- Many trials don't report sex-specific enrollment
- Several all-male or male-skewed cohorts

### 6. Insurance/Financial Barriers
- Industry trials may require specific insurance or distant-site travel
- Access barrier for low-income populations

### 7. Phase Gap
- 96% of trials are NA/Unknown phase
- Very few prosthetic interventions undergo rigorous later-stage testing
